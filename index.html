Frequently Asked Questions

+ What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on all our products. You can return items within 30 days of receipt for a full refund.

+ How long does shipping take?

Shipping typically takes 5-7 business days for standard delivery. Expedited shipping options are available at checkout.

+ Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination.

+ How can I track my order?

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number and a link to track your package online.

+ What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay.

+ What is your rturn policy?

Our return policy allows you to return products within 30 days of purchase. Items must be in their original condition and packaging. For more details, visit our return policy page.

+ Do you offer custom design services?

Yes, we offer custom design services to meet your specific needs. Our team of designers will work with you to create the perfect interior for your space. Contact us for more information.

+ What materials are used in your furniture?

We use high-quality, sustainable materials in all our furniture. Our products are crafted from solid wood, premium fabrics, and eco-friendly finishes to ensure durability and style.

+ How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary based on your location and the product ordered. Typically, our delivery times range from 5 to 10 business days. We will provide you with a tracking number once your order has shipped.
